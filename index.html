About Us

About Nkay Integrated Services

Nkay Integrated Services is a trusted manufacturing company operating from Uvwie, Delta State. We specialize in delivering comprehensive integrated manufacturing solutions that meet the diverse needs of businesses across Nigeria.

Our facility at 7 Kodesoh Street, off Sokoh Road, Effurun, serves as our hub for innovation and excellence. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and reliability in every project we undertake.

With a focus on technical precision and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation as a dependable partner for businesses seeking manufacturing excellence.
